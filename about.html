About Darkoak Essentials

Darkoak Essentials is a Minecraft Bedrock project that brings together quality addons, tools, and documentation for both creators and players.

Our Mission

We aim to make addon creation, world management, and server development easy, stable, and enjoyable for everyone.

  • ⚙️ Tools built for Bedrock developers
  • 📚 Clear documentation
  • 🤝 Community support
